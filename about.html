SINCE 1946

The Beginning of a Timeless Legacy

In 1946, Christian Dior founded his fashion house in Paris with a vision that redefined elegance. Just one year later, his revolutionary "New Look" captivated the world, celebrating femininity with graceful silhouettes, luxurious fabrics, and exceptional craftsmanship.

From a single couture collection to an international symbol of luxury, the House of Dior continues to inspire generations through creativity, innovation, and timeless beauty.
